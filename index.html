Support & FAQ

How do I get started?

Download Artedex, create an account, and allow location access. You'll see art pieces on the map around you.

The map shows no art near me

Artedex currently has the best coverage in Taipei, NYC, Boston, Seattle, and San Francisco. We're adding more cities regularly.

How do I collect a piece?

Walk within range of an art piece, then open the camera to take a photo. The piece will be added to your collection.

Privacy Policy

Artedex ("we", "our", "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we handle it.

Data We Collect

  • Location (always while using the app) — your GPS coordinates are used to show nearby art on the map and to enable AR-guided navigation to art pieces.
  • Photos — when you photograph an art piece to add it to your collection, the photo is uploaded and stored on our servers (AWS S3).
  • Email address & username — collected during account registration for authentication and account identification.

How We Use Your Data

  • Location data is processed in real time to display nearby art and provide navigation. We do not build location history profiles.
  • Photos are associated with your collection and displayed only to you.
  • Your email is used for login, password resets, and essential service communications.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secured infrastructure (DigitalOcean for account data, AWS S3 for photos) with encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. The app does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing artedex@ankycheng.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Artedex is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, email artedex@ankycheng.com.
